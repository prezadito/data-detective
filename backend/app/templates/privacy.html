Privacy Policy

Last Updated: {{ current_year }}-01-15

At Data Detective Academy, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users, especially students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform.

By using Data Detective Academy, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Role (student or teacher)

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • SQL challenge attempts and submissions
  • Progress and completion data
  • Points earned and leaderboard rankings
  • Hints accessed
  • Login times and session duration

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our educational services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Generate leaderboards and analytics
  • Allow teachers to monitor student progress
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to support requests
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • HTTPS: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls limit who can view data
  • Authentication: JWT token-based authentication with short-lived access tokens
  • Regular Updates: We keep our systems and dependencies up to date

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Student Privacy & COPPA Compliance

Protection of Student Information

We take student privacy seriously and comply with applicable student privacy laws, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act).

Student Data Usage

Student data is used solely for educational purposes:

  • We do not sell or rent student information to third parties
  • We do not use student data for advertising or marketing
  • Student data is only accessible to the student, their teachers, and authorized school administrators
  • Parents and guardians have the right to review and request deletion of their child's data

Parental Consent

For students under 13, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information. Schools and teachers act as agents for parents in providing consent when using Data Detective Academy as part of their curriculum.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Improve platform performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our platform.

6. Third-Party Services

Data Detective Academy is a self-hosted platform and does not rely on third-party services for core functionality. When we do use third-party services, we ensure they comply with applicable privacy laws and have appropriate data protection measures in place.

We do not share student data with third parties except as required by law or with explicit consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@datadetectiveacademy.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Data Detective Academy after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
