About Data Detective Academy

Empowering the next generation of data scientists through interactive, gamified SQL education

Our Mission

At Data Detective Academy, we believe that every student deserves access to high-quality computer science education. Our mission is to make SQL learning accessible, engaging, and fun for students in grades 4-8.

Through our detective-themed gamified platform, we transform abstract database concepts into exciting mysteries that students are eager to solve. We're committed to building confident, capable young data scientists who will shape the future of technology.
