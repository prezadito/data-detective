Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What's the difference between Freedom Edition and paid plans?

The Freedom Edition is our fully open-source, self-hosted version licensed under AGPL-3.0. You get all the core features but need to host and maintain it yourself. Paid plans include managed cloud hosting, advanced analytics, priority support, and additional enterprise features like SSO integration and custom branding.

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! You can start with the Freedom Edition to test all the core features. You can also request a 30-day trial of the School plan to experience managed hosting and advanced features. Contact our sales team to get started.

How is billing handled?

School plans are billed annually at $499/year. District plans have custom pricing based on your specific needs and are typically billed annually as well. We accept payment via invoice, credit card, or purchase order.

What happens if we exceed the 500 student limit on the School plan?

If you approach the 500 student limit, we'll contact you to discuss upgrading to a District plan or purchasing additional student capacity. We'll work with you to ensure uninterrupted service for your students.

Can we customize challenges or create our own?

Custom challenge development is available exclusively on District plans. Our team will work with you to create challenges tailored to your curriculum and learning objectives. With the Freedom Edition, you can modify challenges yourself since it's open-source.

Is data migration supported if we upgrade plans?

Yes! If you're upgrading from Freedom Edition to a paid plan, we provide free migration assistance to transfer your student data, progress records, and settings. Our support team will guide you through the entire process.

Do you offer discounts for non-profit organizations?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit educational organizations. Contact our sales team with your non-profit documentation to learn about available discounts.

What kind of support is included?

Freedom Edition users have access to community support via GitHub. School plan includes priority email support with 24-hour response time. District plans get dedicated account managers with 4-hour response times and optional phone support.

Ready to Get Started?

Whether you're a single teacher or a large school district, we have a plan that fits your needs. Contact our sales team to discuss the best option for you.
