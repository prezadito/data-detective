Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the login page by clicking "Forgot Password" and following the instructions sent to your email.

Can I use Data Detective Academy with my entire class?

Absolutely! Teachers can create accounts and import entire class rosters. Check out our School Edition for enhanced features and analytics.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Data Detective Academy is a web-based platform optimized for desktop and tablet browsers. A mobile app is on our roadmap for future development.

How can I suggest new features?

We love hearing from our community! You can submit feature requests via this contact form, through GitHub issues, or by emailing us directly.
